Instructions

How to Play

Start the Game

  • Choose an artist and song, or select random.
  • Click Start to begin.
  • A timer will start automatically.

Make Guesses

  • Type a word into the input box
  • Your guess is checked as you type (no need to press enter).
  • If correct:
    • All matching words in the lyrics are revealed.
    • Your score increases.

What Counts as Correct

  • Words are:
    • Case-insensitive
    • Punctuation is ignored (e.g., "don't" → "dont")
    • You can also get credit for synonyms (if applicable in the system)

Winning the Game

You win when you correctly guess all unique words. A win screen will show your final score and song info.

Running Out of Time

When time expires, the game ends automatically. All missing words are revealed in red and your final score is shown.

Play Again

After finishing, you can play another song or continue with the same artist.

Game Mechanics

Understand the Board

  • The lyrics are hidden as blanks (______).
  • Each blank represents a word in the song.
  • Words are revealed as you guess them correctly.

Score System

  • Your score = number of unique words guessed
  • Total score = total unique words in the song
  • Displayed as: score / total

Timer & Controls

  • Pause: Stops the timer and blurs lyrics
  • Continue: Continue the game
  • Stop: Ends the game immediately and reveals all lyrics
  • Reset: Restart the game
